Finish the game in record time, and you’ll walk away with your very own Hit Kit. The Hit Kit comes complete with a Scrubber, three brush heads, and Rubbermaid gloves. HOW TO PLAY

Begin by identifying the spots that must be cleaned in the first room. These spots can be found on your Hit List.

Choose the correct brush head needed to scrub away each spot on the list, certain heads clean certain dirts.

Eliminate all the spots within a certain amount of time, and you may proceed to the next room.

Be thorough, but move quickly. You’ll only have three minutes to complete all three rooms.
